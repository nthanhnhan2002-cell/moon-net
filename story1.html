<h1>The Fisherman Who Caught the Moon</h1>


<p>

One night, a fisherman cast his net into the quiet sea.

</p>


<p>

When he pulled it back, it was glowing.

Not with fish… but with moonlight.

</p>


<p>

The moon had fallen into his net.

</p>


<p>

He did not keep it.

</p>


<p>

Instead, he hung the glowing net beside his bed

so that every night the moon could visit his dreams again.
